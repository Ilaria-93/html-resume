Ilaria Faleschini

SUMMARY

My training began in Udine, at the scientific high school, and developed in parallel between the Conservatory of Bologna and the school of musical specialization in Fiesole. Subsequently, between 2013 and 2016, I had the opportunity to study at the Hochschule für Musik Detmold in Germany, recognized as one of the best European universities for Performing Arts. During this time, I worked with several German professional orchestras as a violist, taking part in international tours.
In 2019 I obtained a Master in Arts from the prestigious Guildhall University, ranked among the top ten institutions in the world for Performing Arts. Since 2014, I have collaborated with some of the best European orchestras as an orchestra player, including the London Symphony Orchestra and the Lucerne Festival Orchestra.
At the same time, I had the opportunity to attend a training course with the CFO and Vice Chancellor of Guildhall University. This experience allowed me to be selected first by Sony for an internship, and then in 2020 I joined one of the top 3 international agencies in the music business, managing clients and projects on various aspects and many areas.
Today, as Project Manager, I support the team in the provision of services in various sectors, contributing to multidisciplinary projects. I support clients of international importance.
My academic and professional career has made me a multifaceted professional, ready to handle complex challenges in multiple cultural and linguistic contexts.
